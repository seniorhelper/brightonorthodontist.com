About the Doctor

A Brighton Orthodontist Who Puts People First

Dr. Rex Johnson has built something rare in modern dentistry. He has built a practice where people actually matter. Rex Johnson Orthodontics is not a corporate chain. It is not a franchise. It is a hometown orthodontic office — right here in Brighton, Colorado — where patients are treated like neighbors, because that is exactly what they are.

Furthermore, when you come to Rex Johnson Orthodontics, you see Dr. Johnson. Every time. He personally reviews your records, monitors your progress, and makes every treatment decision from start to finish. That kind of continuity is hard to find today. And yet, it is the standard here.

"Every patient deserves the same attentive, personalized care I would want for my own family."

Education Built on Excellence

Dr. Johnson's clinical foundation is strong. He earned his Doctorate in Dentistry from the University of Nebraska — one of the Midwest's most respected dental programs. He then went on to complete a highly competitive orthodontic specialty residency at the University of Minnesota, which is consistently recognized as one of the top programs in the country.

As a result, Dr. Johnson entered private practice with both the technical depth and clinical confidence that Brighton families deserve. Additionally, he has never stopped learning. He regularly attends continuing education courses so that his patients always benefit from the most current techniques and technology available in orthodontics today.

Technology That Makes a Difference

Modern orthodontics has changed dramatically. Therefore, Rex Johnson Orthodontics has kept pace. The practice uses digital records, advanced digital imaging, and 3D computer imaging to plan and execute treatment with exceptional precision.

In practice, this means fewer surprises. It means shorter treatment times. And it means better outcomes — because Dr. Johnson can see exactly what is happening before he ever adjusts a wire or hands you a new aligner tray.

For patients interested in a more discreet option, Dr. Johnson is also a certified Invisalign provider. Clear aligners have changed the game for adults and teens throughout the Brighton, CO area who want to straighten their teeth without disrupting daily life. Not every patient is a candidate, however. Dr. Johnson evaluates each case individually to determine which treatment will produce the best long-term result.

A Practice Built Around Families

Rex Johnson Orthodontics welcomes patients of all ages. So whether you're bringing in a seven-year-old for a first evaluation or scheduling your own adult consultation, you are in the right place.

Interestingly, approximately one in five orthodontic patients in the United States today is an adult. That number keeps growing. The reason is simple: options like Invisalign and clear ceramic braces make treatment far more discreet and manageable for busy adults. Consequently, more Brighton-area adults are finally getting the smile they have always wanted.

For younger patients, early evaluation matters. The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a first orthodontic screening by age seven. At that age, Dr. Johnson can identify developing jaw issues, crowding, or bite problems while the jaw is still growing and most responsive to treatment. Moreover, early evaluation does not always mean early braces. Often, it simply means having the right information to make smart decisions at the right time.
