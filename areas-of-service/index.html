What We Treat — Services Available to All Communities

Every patient who walks through our door — whether from Brighton, Hudson, or Wattenberg — has access to the full range of orthodontic treatments Dr. Johnson offers. Consequently, you never have to compromise on options just because of where you live.

  • 🔩
    Traditional Metal Braces — The most effective and affordable option, ideal for any age and most case types.
  • 💎
