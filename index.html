Treatment Options

Brighton Orthodontic Services for Every Age & Lifestyle

Dr. Johnson offers multiple orthodontic solutions so your treatment fits your life — whether you're a seven-year-old, a busy teen athlete, or an adult professional looking for a discreet option.

🔩

Traditional Metal Braces

The gold standard of orthodontics — and more comfortable than ever. Modern metal brackets are smaller, lighter, and more efficient than those from even ten years ago. Traditional braces are ideal for complex cases and are the most cost-effective option available from your Brighton orthodontist.

💎

Clear Ceramic Braces

Same precise tooth movement as metal braces, but with tooth-colored ceramic brackets that blend in with your smile. A popular choice for teens and adults who want effective treatment with a lower profile appearance. Ask Dr. Johnson if clear ceramic braces are right for your case.

✨

Invisalign® Clear Aligners

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, removable clear trays to gradually reposition your teeth — no brackets, no wires. Virtually invisible and easy to maintain, Invisalign is popular with teens and adults in Brighton who want to straighten their teeth without interrupting daily life.

👶

Children's Early Orthodontics

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a first orthodontic evaluation by age seven. Early treatment, sometimes called Phase 1, can guide jaw development, create space for permanent teeth, and reduce the complexity and cost of later treatment.

🧑

Teen Orthodontics

Teen patients are in the most common treatment window for orthodontics. Dr. Johnson offers braces and Invisalign Teen, both designed to work with active teen lifestyles — including sports, music, and school activities. Mouthguard recommendations are included.

🛡️

Retention & Retainers

Finishing treatment is just the beginning. Your retainer is what preserves your results for a lifetime. Dr. Johnson will prescribe the right retainer — fixed, removable, or a combination — and explain exactly how long and how often to wear it to protect your investment.
