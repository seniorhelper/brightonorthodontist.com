Fun for Every Kid in the Waiting Room

At Rex Johnson Orthodontics, we believe your visit should be something to look forward to — not just something to sit through. That's why we built Smile Games: a collection of fun, kid-friendly activities you can play right here on any phone, tablet, or computer while you wait.

Draw your own masterpiece on our digital canvas. Challenge yourself with orthodontics-themed crossword puzzles or Sudoku brain-teasers. Learn your ABCs or test your math skills at your own level. Flip through surprising smile facts that even most adults don't know. Or take our Smile Trivia quiz and see how much you know about teeth, braces, and Dr. Rex's practice!

Whether you're seven years old or seventeen, there's something here for you. Pick a game below and jump right in. No download needed — just tap and play. 🎉
